# Website Design: Divya N Modi

This design is crafted to convey trust, professionalism, and modern efficiency using GSAP for high-end interaction and Tailwind CSS for clean styling.

## 1. Visual Identity & Palette
*   **Primary Color:** Deep Navy Blue (#1A2B48) - Represents trust, wisdom, and the legal profession.
*   **Secondary Color:** Gold/Bronze (#D4AF37) - Represents excellence, quality, and legacy.
*   **Accent Color:** Slate Grey (#708090) - For subtle text and background variations.
*   **Base Color:** Soft White / Off-White (#F8F9FA) - To keep the design clean and readable.
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headings:** Playfair Display (Serif) - To convey tradition and prestige.
    *   **Body:** Montserrat (Sans-Serif) - For clarity and modern readability.

## 2. Interaction & Motion Design (GSAP)
*   **Hero Reveal:** Smooth fade-in and slide-up of the value proposition and CTA buttons upon page load.
*   **Scroll Reveal:** Sections should gently slide in from the left/right or fade up as the user scrolls to keep them engaged (similar to high-end law firm sites like Khaitan & Co).
*   **Service Card Hover:** Subtle lift effect and color shift on specialization cards to indicate interactivity.
*   **Smooth Scroll:** Implementing Lenis or GSAP ScrollSmoother for a premium navigation feel.
*   **Counter Animation:** Numbers for "29+ Years" and "200+ Articles" should count up when they come into view.

## 3. UI Components & Layout
*   **Clean Grid Layout:** Use CSS Grid for the Practice Areas section to ensure perfect alignment and responsiveness.
*   **High-Quality Imagery:** Use professional photos of Divya N Modi (if available) or high-quality, non-cliché legal stock (books, gavels, clean office spaces).
*   **Readable Typography:** Large line heights (1.6+) and ample white space to prevent "wall of text" syndrome.
*   **Trust Icons:** Use professional, thin-line icons (not chunky generic ones) for practice areas.
*   **Form Design:** Minimalist contact forms with clear floating labels and success animations.

## 4. Mobile UX (Critical)
*   **Thumb-Friendly Buttons:** Large CTAs for calling and WhatsApp.
*   **Bottom Navigation:** (Optional) A quick-access bar at the bottom for Home, Practice Areas, and Call on mobile devices.
*   **Fast LCP (Largest Contentful Paint):** Optimize the hero image to ensure it loads instantly on mobile networks.

## 5. Trust Signals Placement
*   **Bar Council Logo:** Placed subtly in the footer or header to verify professional standing.
*   **Notary Seal (Stylized):** Used as a graphic element in the Notary services section to emphasize official status.
*   **Publication Logos:** Mentioning "Divya Bhaskar" in the Articles section to build authority.
