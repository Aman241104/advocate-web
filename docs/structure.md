# Website Structure: Divya N Modi

This structure combines best practices from Khaitan & Co (for corporate authority), Candour Legal (for modern UI), and Advocate Ahmedabad (for local SEO).

## 1. Global Components
*   **Sticky Header:**
    *   Logo (Divya N Modi Advocate & Notary)
    *   Nav: Home, About, Practice Areas, Notary, Articles, Contact.
    *   Call CTA Button (Mobile friendly).
*   **Sticky Contact (Floating):**
    *   WhatsApp Button (Bottom Right).
    *   Back to Top (Bottom Left).
*   **Footer:**
    *   Quick Links
    *   Social Media Icons (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram)
    *   Contact Info (Phone, Address, Email)
    *   Bar Council of India Disclaimer (Mandatory).

## 2. Pages & Sections

### **Homepage**
1.  **Hero Section:**
    *   High-quality image of the advocate (or professional law-related imagery).
    *   Value Proposition: "29 Years of Legal Excellence and Trusted Advocacy."
    *   Primary CTA: "Schedule a Consultation."
2.  **Quick Bio / About Section:**
    *   Highlighting "Divya N Modi, Advocate & Notary."
    *   Mention 29 years experience and 200+ articles.
3.  **Specialization Overview (Grid):**
    *   Cards for Matrimonial, Property, Corporate, Notary, etc.
    *   "View All Practice Areas" button.
4.  **Trust Signals (Counter):**
    *   29+ Years Experience | 50+ MNC Clients | 200+ Published Articles.
5.  **Notary Services Section:**
    *   Highlighting Notary services specifically since 2008.
6.  **Articles & Insights (Carousel):**
    *   Snippet of the latest "Adhikar" column articles.
7.  **Client Testimonials:**
    *   Google Reviews and personal testimonials.
8.  **Contact Form / CTA:**
    *   Direct inquiry form and map.

### **About Page**
*   In-depth biography.
*   Educational background.
*   Philosophy of legal practice (Ethical, Result-oriented).
*   Bar Council registration details.

### **Practice Areas (Parent Page + Subpages)**
*   **Family Law:** (Divorce, Adoption, Marriage)
*   **Property Law:** (Title deeds, Land disputes)
*   **Corporate Law:** (MNC agreements, Legal opinions)
*   **Litigation:** (Cheque bounce, Consumer forum, MCOP)
*   *Each subpage will have a dedicated SEO-friendly URL.*

### **Notary & Affidavits**
*   List of specific documents handled.
*   Process for Notary services.
*   Verification and secrecy standards.

### **Articles/Blog**
*   Archive of "Adhikar" column articles.
*   Legal tips for common issues (How to register marriage, What to do for cheque bounce).

### **Contact Us**
*   Interactive Map.
*   Detailed Address.
*   Click-to-call phone numbers.
*   Consultation request form.

## 3. SEO Structure
*   **URL Strategy:** `/practice-areas/divorce-lawyer-ahmedabad`
*   **Breadcrumbs:** To help user navigation and search engine understanding.
*   **Schema Markup:** `LegalService` schema on homepage, `Article` schema on blog posts.
